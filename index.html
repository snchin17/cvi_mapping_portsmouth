Portsmouth Climate Vulnerability Index Maps

This page provides access to a series of interactive maps developed to assess heat-related vulnerability across Portsmouth at the LSOA level. The maps highlight key heat hazard indicators such as temperature extremes and the frequency of warm or overheat days, alongside a composite vulnerability index. Click any button below to open the corresponding map in a new tab and explore local patterns in climate risk.
